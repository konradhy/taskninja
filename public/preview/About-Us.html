    HISTORY

    Our (super brief) Journey

    1988

    The shift in culture

    The way we live, work and communicate has changed dramatically over the past decade. These transformations are intertwined and a direct result of the rapid improvements in technology. The characteristics coveted while job searching have also changed. Today many of us are interested in having a higher level of autonomy, job flexibility and customization in our careers. 

    At the same time, consumers have begun to prioritise convenience, reliability and variety when buying goods and services. And they have become accustomed to relying on technology to ensure they get the most mileage out of their dollar. 

    1991

    The Development

    Task Ninja was conceptualized and created in response to this cultural change. The Task Ninja platform seeks to do two things:

    Make it easy for trained professionals to leverage technology to supercharge their business. The platform makes it easy for them to find customers, accept online payments, schedule appointments, monitor their income and more. Consequently, they get the chance to focus on their craft
     
    Make it easy for the customer to use technology to find the perfect professional for their task. The platform allows you to search through a large catalogue of workers based on price, rating, location and category. 

    2003

    The Launch

    Task Ninja was made by Jamaicans and (currently) only available for Jamaicans. The platform is currently target towards providing gardeners, electricians, photographers, house cleaners, tradesmen, and the like. 

    Customers are able to schedule appointments, pay via card or PayPal, and leave reviews and ratings about their experience.  

    2015

    The future

    Task Ninja will eventually be available to other Caribbean islands. It will include additional categories such as lawyers, doctors and architects. And it will boast additional features such as being able to instantly chat with your provider and by providing a suite of back end tools that help the providers manage their business.
