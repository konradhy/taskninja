    It's That Easy

    Register

    Step 1 is to register. Make sure you put in an email address and phone number that you have access to. That'll be important later on.

    Tell us about the job

    Step 3 is to tell us about the job. If you're having trouble moving forward, then it's worth double checking to ensure that each field was filled out properly

    Choose Your Task

    Step 2 is to choose your task. You can do this by typing in the search bar on the front page, or by scrolling through all available tasks by clicking tasks in the menu bar

    Choose your Ninja

    Finally you can either choose your own Ninja for the job, or you can leave it open and allow them to decide among themselves. 

    If you have any questions click the rectangular help button on the right of the page or click that blue chat button to speak with someone from our team. 

    How Task Ninja Works for the Ninjas

    Apply

    Click here and fill out the form to apply. Ensure that you fill out the form accurately and honestly. 

    Attend Interview

    A member from the Task Ninja team will review your application, credentials, reference etc and will reach out to you for an interview. 

    Accept Task

    Once you're a team member you will be able to accept tasks. Be on the look out for jobs that are emailed to you. You can also find jobs through the Ninja Dashboard.

    Complete Tasks

    Complete all jobs that you were assinged on time and to the best of your ability. You can view your jobs by going to the Ninja Dashboard and then clicking your jobs. Keep the client updated while you're completing the job, and set estimates through the dashboard.

    Accept Payment

    Accept payment for the job using whatever method the client is comfortable with. Depending on the type of job Task Ninja will be entitled to a percentage of your earnings. 


    Review Client

    Leave a review about the client and your experience working with them.



    Follow Us

    We pride ourselves in connecting you to high quality professionals. If you are unsatisfied in any way please don't hesitate to contact us.
